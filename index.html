In this first lesson, we will learn about tags. Tags are hidden keywords that tell the browser what a description is about. Tags are pre-defined, so you cannot make your own tags. :( You can think of tags as a human body. The first tag is Doctype. You can think of Doctype as a cap. Since you wear a cap on top of your head, Doctype comes before Head in HTML.

Hi guys, in our next lesson, we will be learning about the basics of html. In HTML, there are many tags that you can use.

Here are some examples of this.

  • p=paragraphs
  • h1 to h6=header
  • !DOCTYPE=document type
  • title=title
  • body=body
  • ol=ordered list
  • ul=unordered list
  • li=list item
